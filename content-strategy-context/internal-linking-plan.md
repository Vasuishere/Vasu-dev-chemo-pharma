## 1. TOPICAL AUTHORITY ARCHITECTURE (Hub & Spoke Model)

### What is Topical Authority?
Google ranks websites higher when they demonstrate deep expertise on a topic. Instead of writing isolated pages, we build **content clusters** — a pillar page (hub) surrounded by related supporting pages (spokes) — all interlinked. This tells Google: "Vasudev Chemo Pharma is THE authority on MEA Triazine 78% and H2S scavenging."

## 4. SEMANTIC CONTENT WRITING STRATEGY

### What is Semantic Content?
Semantic content writing means covering a topic so thoroughly that Google understands your page answers ALL related queries — not just the exact keyword. You use related terms, synonyms, entities, and contextual phrases naturally.

### 4.1 Semantic Keyword Clusters per Page Type

#### Product Pillar Page (`/supply/mea-triazine-78`):

**Primary Entity:** MEA Triazine 78%

**Semantic Terms to Include (naturally in body text):**
- Chemical names: Hexahydro-1,3,5-tris(hydroxyethyl)-s-triazine, monoethanolamine triazine, 1,3,5-triazine derivative
- Identifiers: CAS 4719-04-4, EC 225-208-0
- Process terms: H2S scavenging, hydrogen sulfide removal, gas sweetening, sour gas treatment, desulfurization
- Properties: non-regenerative scavenger, water-soluble, biodegradable, non-corrosive
- Synthesis: monoethanolamine + formaldehyde reaction
- Byproduct: dithiazine (spent triazine)
- Packaging: 200L drum, 1000L IBC tote, ISO tank, bulk tanker
- Concentrations: 78% active content, 80%, 60%, 30% (mention variants)
- Certifications: ISO 9001:2015, REACH compliant, TSCA listed
- Manufacturer language: direct manufacturer, factory pricing, made in India, quality assurance, batch consistency
- Competitor brand equivalents: "equivalent to Grotan BK, BASOLON SC 78, Pro3, PureMark" (positions Vasudev as the direct-source alternative)

#### Country Pages — Semantic Writing Formula:

Each country page must weave in these semantic layers:

1. **Product Entity** — MEA Triazine 78%, H2S scavenger, triazine scavenger
2. **Geographic Entity** — Country name, major cities, ports, industrial zones
3. **Industry Entity** — National oil company name, major fields, refineries
4. **Regulatory Entity** — Import regulations, certifications, compliance bodies
5. **Logistics Entity** — Port names, transit times, packaging, Incoterms (CIF, FOB)
6. **Language Entity** — Native-language terms for the product/application
7. **Commercial Entity** — Bulk supply, wholesale, competitive pricing, direct manufacturer

**Example Semantic Paragraph (UAE page):**

> Vasudev Chemo Pharma is a **direct manufacturer of MEA Triazine 78%** (CAS 4719-04-4) supplying the **UAE oil and gas industry**. Our **H2S scavenger** is used by operators across **Abu Dhabi and Dubai** for **sour gas treatment** in upstream and midstream operations. We supply through **Jebel Ali Port (JAFZA)** and **Khalifa Port** with a transit time of just **3-5 days from India**. Our product meets **ADNOC vendor qualification** standards and complies with **Abu Dhabi EHS regulations**. Available in **200L drums and 1000L IBC totes** — also known regionally as **مزيل كبريتيد الهيدروجين** (H2S remover) or **كيماويات النفط والغاز** (oil and gas chemicals).

This single paragraph covers: product, geography, industry, logistics, regulatory, language, and commercial entities.

## 7. INTERNAL LINKING RULES (Apply Everywhere)

### Rule 1: Every page links to the Product Pillar
Every single page on the site must contain at least one link to `/supply/mea-triazine-78`. This is non-negotiable for building topical authority.

### Rule 2: Maximum 3 clicks to any page
No page should be more than 3 clicks from the homepage:
- Click 1: Homepage → Pillar page
- Click 2: Pillar page → Spoke page
- Click 3: Spoke page → Related spoke page

### Rule 3: Contextual anchor text only
Never use "click here" or "read more". Always use keyword-rich, descriptive anchor text:
- ✅ "MEA Triazine 78% for oil and gas operations"
- ✅ "H2S scavenger supply to UAE"
- ❌ "click here to learn more"
- ❌ "read more"

### Rule 4: Link in the body, not just sidebars
Google values in-content links more than navigation/sidebar links. Every page must have 2-3 links within the body text itself.

### Rule 5: First mention gets the link
When a keyword appears multiple times on a page, only the FIRST mention should be hyperlinked. Don't over-link.

### Rule 6: No orphan pages
Every page must be linked FROM at least 2 other pages. Use the sitemap below to verify.

### Rule 7: Vary anchor text
Don't use the exact same anchor text for every link to the same page. Vary it:
- "MEA Triazine 78%"
- "our flagship H2S scavenger"
- "MEA Triazine 78% by Vasudev Chemo Pharma"
- "the industry-standard triazine H2S scavenger"
